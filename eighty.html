Thriller

Michael Jackson

1982

Michael Jackson's 'Thriller' revolutionized the music industry with its groundbreaking sound and iconic music video. This infectious track, with its hauntingly catchy chorus and Vincent Price's eerie narration, became a cultural phenomenon, firmly establishing Michael Jackson as the King of Pop and leaving an enduring legacy in the world of music and entertainment. The monologue at the end of the track always frightened me as a child.



Material Girl

Madonna

1984

Madonna's 'Material Girl' is a quintessential '80s anthem that embodies the era's materialistic ethos and Madonna's own image of glamour and independence. Classic 80's girl anthem.



Livin on a Prayer

Bon Jovi

1986

Bon Jovi's 'Livin' on a Prayer' is a timeless rock anthem that captures the resilience of blue-collar America. The song's powerful message of love and determination in the face of adversity struck a chord with audiences worldwide.



No Sleep Till Brooklyn

The Beastie Boys

1986

The Beastie Boys' 'No Sleep Till Brooklyn' is a high-octane hip-hop classic that oozes attitude and irreverence. With its relentless energy, clever wordplay, and an unforgettable guitar riff, the song became a defining track in the Beastie Boys' career and a symbol of the rebellious spirit of hip-hop and rock fusion.



I Wanna Dance with Somebody by Whitney Houston

Whitney Houston

1987

Whitney Houston's 'I Wanna Dance with Somebody' is a dynamic dance-pop classic that shines a spotlight on her extraordinary vocal prowess. With Whitney's unmatched ability to effortlessly transition between soaring highs and soulful depths, she infuses the song with an unparalleled emotional depth and pure, raw talent. It just makes you wanna scream and sing along.



Fast Car

Tracy Chapman

1988

Tracy Chapman's 'Fast Car' is a poignant and raw folk-rock ballad that tells a heart-wrenching story of dreams and escape. Chapman's soulful vocals and the song's stripped-down instrumentation create a powerful emotional resonance You could listen to her voice for hours.
