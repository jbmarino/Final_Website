Let Me Tell You About the Website I Created

Hello! This is my final project for my class SI 339: Web Design, Development, and Accessibility. For this project, I've wanted to create a website that celebrates music that has left a profound impact on me. I've curated a collection of songs from the 1960s through the 1990s.


To me, music is not just an assortment of notes and melodies; it's a powerful form of storytelling. It serves as a mirror, reflecting the experiences, emotions, and creative expressions of both the artists who create it and the listeners who connect with it on a personal level.


I've strived to create an accessible and engaging platform for music enthusiasts and anyone who appreciates the storytelling power of songs. This website is not just a collection of tracks; it's a journey through time, a tribute to the artists who've shaped our musical landscape, and a reflection of the personal emotions and feelings that music can evoke.
